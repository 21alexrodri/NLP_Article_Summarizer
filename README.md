# NLP_Article_Summarizer

A project to analyze, classify, and summarize news articles using **NLP** techniques. This project combines **Newspaper**, **BeautifulSoup**, **NLTK**, **Spacy**, and **Hugging Face** models to automatically generate summaries and thematic classifications of articles.

## Sample Articles

The following articles are used as examples to test the pipeline:

1. [Grasshopper Genome Expansion](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s12915-020-00925-x) – Scientific article on insect genomics.  
2. [Barcelona VAR Controversy](https://en.nogomania.com/read/Was-it-really-offside-Barca-rage-after-controversial-decision-in-San-Sebastian#goog_rewarded) – Sports news about a football match.  
3. [Gold vs Dollar Index Fallacy](https://goldbroker.com/news/fallacy-inverse-relationship-between-gold-dollar-index-dxy-3363) – Financial article on the behavior of the dollar and gold.  
4. [Albino Gorilla Dies in Spain](https://www.nbcnews.com/id/wbna3541441) – Nature and science news about an albino gorilla.  
5. [Spain Train Disaster Coverage](https://www.ap.org/the-definitive-source/behind-the-news/spain-train-crash-how-a-journalists-quick-thinking-led-to-vital-info/) – Article on a train accident and journalistic coverage.
